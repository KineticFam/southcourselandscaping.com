Native Plant Installation

The right plants, sourced well, and installed properly. That's really what it comes down to.

How We Approach It

We work with trusted native nurseries to get healthy, well-grown plants that are suited to our local conditions. Every plant is chosen not just because it's native, but because it's right for your specific spot—your shade level, soil type, and salt exposure. Then we install it properly so it establishes strong roots and thrives.

Why It Matters Here

Native plants aren't just good for the environment—they're practical. Cocoplums handle salt without flinching. Live oaks are built for hurricane winds. Bald cypress love our wet summers. These plants create the shade and beauty South Florida is known for, without the constant watering and chemical treatments that exotics need.

Good For

  • • Replacing plants that keep dying or struggling
  • • Adding shade, privacy, or color with native species
  • • Properties that need to handle storms and drought
  • • Anyone who wants a lower-maintenance yard

Tree Installation & Relocation

Big trees make a big difference. We handle the heavy lifting—literally—so you get shade and character without waiting decades.

How We Approach It

Whether we're sourcing a specimen tree or relocating one on your property, we handle all the logistics: soil prep, root ball management, equipment, and the careful post-install care that makes sure the tree takes hold and thrives in its new spot.

Property Renovation

Your yard isn't a lost cause—it probably just has the wrong plants. We can fix that.

How We Approach It

We start by figuring out what's actually going on—soil issues, drainage problems, plants that just don't belong here. Ongoing Maintenance

We keep your landscape healthy and looking sharp—with care that works with native plant cycles, not against them.

How We Approach It

Native plants have their own rhythms—when they flush new growth, when to prune, when to leave them alone. Our maintenance follows those cycles. We do strategic pruning that keeps natural form, fertilize to build soil health, and manage irrigation to encourage deep roots. The goal is a landscape that needs less intervention over time, not more.

Why It Matters Here

Most maintenance crews treat every plant the same. But knowing when cocoplums flush new growth, how to prune palms for storm resistance, and which native groundcovers to encourage versus manage—that takes specific knowledge. Good native maintenance actually reduces your long-term costs while keeping everything looking better.

Good For

  • • Properties with established native landscapes
  • • Homeowners who want reliable, consistent care
  • • Properties with mature trees that need expert attention
  • • Anyone who wants their yard to look great without the hassle

How We Work Together

Clear steps, clear communication, no surprises. Here's what working with us looks like.

1. We Come Out and Listen

We visit your property, walk the site, look at the soil and conditions, and most importantly—hear what you want. We'll share what we see and talk through what's possible with native plants in your specific situation.

2. We Put Together a Plan

Based on your input and our site assessment, we create a clear plan with plant selections, layout, and honest pricing. Every recommendation has a reason behind it—we'll explain our thinking.

3. We Do the Work

Our crew shows up on time and works clean. Proper soil prep, careful planting, and thorough cleanup. We keep you in the loop throughout and don't leave until we're satisfied with the result.

4. We Follow Up

After install, we check in during the establishment period to make sure everything's taking hold. We'll give you care tips and answer any questions that come up. We're not gone after planting day.
