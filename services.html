Native Plant Installation

Sourcing and installing the finest native South Florida specimens—from towering banyans to delicate native groundcovers.

What We Do

We work exclusively with native plant nurseries that understand genetic diversity and local ecotypes. Every plant is selected not just for its species, but for its specific suitability to your microclimate. Installation follows best practices for root establishment, soil amendment, and long-term success.

Why It Matters in Fort Lauderdale

Native plants aren't just environmentally responsible—they're the key to landscapes that thrive in our climate. Cocoplums that handle salt exposure. Live oaks that bend in hurricane winds. Bald cypress that actually prefer our wet summer conditions. These plants create the authentic South Florida aesthetic while requiring minimal irrigation and chemical inputs.

Tree Installation & Relocation

Specialist expertise in moving and establishing mature trees—creating instant canopy and architectural elements that would take decades to grow naturally.

What We Do

From sourcing specimen trees to complex relocation projects, we handle the technical challenges of establishing mature trees in South Florida conditions. This includes soil preparation, root ball management, crane operations, and post-installation care protocols that ensure long-term success.

Why It Matters in Fort Lauderdale

In our climate, mature canopy isn't just beautiful—it's essential for outdoor livability. Large trees provide the cooling effect that makes patios and pools usable year-round. Hurricane-resistant natives like live oaks and bald cypress also provide wind protection and storm resilience that exotic trees cannot match.

Perfect For

  • • Properties requiring instant mature landscape impact
  • • Historic estates maintaining authentic character
  • • New construction needing immediate shade and privacy
  • • Relocating valuable trees during property renovation

Ongoing Landscape Management

Maintenance programs designed around native plant cycles and South Florida's seasonal patterns—keeping your landscape healthy, beautiful, and sustainable.

What We Do

Our management approach works with native plant biology and seasonal cycles. This means strategic pruning that enhances natural form, fertilization programs that support soil health, and irrigation management that encourages deep root development. We maintain beauty while building long-term landscape resilience.

Why It Matters in Fort Lauderdale

Native landscapes require different care than exotic installations. Understanding when cocoplums naturally flush new growth, how to prune palms for hurricane resistance, and which native groundcovers to encourage versus control requires specific expertise. Proper native landscape management actually reduces long-term maintenance needs while improving appearance.

Perfect For

  • • Homeowners seeking reduced maintenance costs
  • • Properties requiring specialized tree care
  • • Estate-level properties demanding premium results

Our Process: From Vision to Reality

Every project begins with understanding your property's unique character and your vision for beautiful outdoor living.

1. Site Analysis & Consultation

Comprehensive assessment of soil conditions, drainage, existing vegetation, microclimates, and architectural character. We listen to your vision and explain what's possible with native South Florida plantings.

2. Design Development

Creating detailed plans that honor your property's natural character while meeting your aesthetic and functional needs. Every plant selection is made for specific reasons—beauty, function, and long-term success.

3. Professional Installation

Meticulous installation following best practices for soil preparation, plant establishment, and long-term success. Our team handles everything from soil amendment to final cleanup.

Ongoing guidance during the critical establishment period. We provide care instructions, monitor plant health, and ensure your new landscape develops into the thriving ecosystem we designed.
