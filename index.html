The Land Remembers

Before the development boom, Fort Lauderdale was a tapestry of native hammocks, coastal dunes, and cypress wetlands. That landscape is in the soil's memory—and ours.

South Course Landscaping was born from a recognition that South Florida's most enduring and beautiful properties honor this natural heritage. While others chase trends, we cultivate timeless landscapes rooted in ecological truth.

Our work graces some of Broward County's most distinguished addresses because we understand that authentic Florida landscaping isn't about forcing exotic plants to survive—it's about letting native species flourish.
