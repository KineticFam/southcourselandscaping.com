Why Choose Native Landscaping?

Long-Term Value

Native landscapes appreciate in beauty and value over time, unlike exotic installations that require constant replacement and renovation.

Climate Resilience

Hurricane-resistant, drought-tolerant species that thrive in South Florida's unique subtropical conditions without artificial life support.

Authentic Beauty

The deep, layered canopy and sophisticated aesthetic that defines South Florida's most prestigious properties.

Ecological Intelligence

Landscapes that support local wildlife, improve air quality, and contribute to the ecological health of our community.

Common Questions

How long does native landscape installation take?

Project timelines vary based on scope, but most residential installations range from 2-6 weeks from design approval to completion. Larger estate projects may require 2-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation process.

Do native plants really require less maintenance?

Once established (typically 12-18 months), native South Florida plants require significantly less water, fertilization, and pest control than exotic species. However, they still benefit from knowledgeable care that understands their natural growth cycles and pruning requirements.

Can native landscaping look sophisticated and designed?

Absolutely. Our work graces some of Fort Lauderdale's most prestigious addresses because we understand that native doesn't mean wild. We create structured, elegant landscapes that happen to use indigenous species—the best of both worlds.

What's the investment for native landscape design?

Investment varies widely based on property size, scope, and plant material selections. We provide detailed proposals after the initial consultation. Many clients find that long-term savings from reduced maintenance costs make native landscaping highly cost-effective.

Do you work with architects and property managers?

Yes, we regularly collaborate with architects, interior designers, and property managers on both residential and commercial projects. We understand the coordination required for successful large-scale projects and new construction.
