Unique Landscape Characteristics

Fort Lauderdale's diverse microclimates range from coastal areas with salt exposure to inland neighborhoods with heavier soils and more protected conditions. Our Experience Here

Salt air exposure, hurricane preparedness, and the aesthetic expectations of luxury waterfront properties demand specialist expertise.

Coral Ridge

Golf course community landscapes that honor the natural beauty of South Florida's inland subtropical environments.

Golf Course Integration

Properties in Coral Ridge benefit from the open space and mature landscaping of the adjacent golf course, but also face unique challenges in creating private landscapes that complement rather than compete with these views. Native plantings provide the perfect solution—species that echo the natural Florida landscape while creating intimate outdoor spaces.

Inland Microclimate

Coral Ridge's location slightly inland from the coast creates different growing conditions than waterfront properties. Less salt exposure but more inland humidity and heat require native species selection that takes advantage of these conditions rather than fighting them. This is where our deep knowledge of South Florida ecology makes the difference.

Coral Ridge Advantages

  • • Larger lots allowing for comprehensive native ecosystems
  • • Golf course views enhanced by complementary native plantings
  • • Mature community infrastructure supporting established landscapes
  • • Inland conditions perfect for heat-loving native species
  • • Community appreciation for sophisticated landscape design

Harbor Beach & Lighthouse Point

Luxury coastal communities requiring specialized knowledge of salt-tolerant native species and hurricane-resistant landscape design.

Coastal Environment Expertise

Oceanfront and near-ocean properties face unique challenges that only native coastal species can truly handle. Salt spray, sandy soils, high winds, and storm surge potential require plant selections and landscape designs that work with these conditions rather than trying to overcome them.

Luxury Landscape Standards

Harbor Beach and Lighthouse Point properties expect landscape beauty that matches their premium locations and architectural investments. Our native designs provide this sophistication while ensuring long-term success in challenging coastal conditions.

Coastal Native Specialists

  • • Sea grape for ultimate salt tolerance and privacy
  • • Cocoplums for coastal hedging and hurricane resistance
  • • Sabal palms for tropical character and wind resistance
  • • Beach sunflower and sea oats for dune stabilization
  • • Native grasses for low-maintenance coastal beauty

Hurricane Preparedness

Our coastal landscape designs prioritize hurricane resistance—species selection that bends without breaking, root systems that provide erosion control, and landscape structures that protect rather than threaten property during storm events.
